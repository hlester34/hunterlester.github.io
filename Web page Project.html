Julio Jones

Atlanta Falcons Wide Receiver

High School and College Years

Born and raised in Foley, Alabama, Julio Jones quickly made it known that he was superstar athlete. He attended Foley High School where he excelled in football, basketball and track, although it was football that he would go on to play in college. After an incredible high school career, he was considered a five star recruit and the number one wide receiver in the country. He was the most sought after wide receiver in the 2008 high school class and was offered a scholarship from numerous powerhouse college football programs across the nation. He chose to attend the University of Alabama where he would become one of the best wide receivers in school history. Over the course of his three year career at Alabama, Jones won a national championship, a SEC Championship, and was first team all SEC. He also became fourth in school history in career touchdown catches, and second in receptions and yards.

NFL Career

Julio Jones decided to leave college after his junior year and enter the NFL draft. Because of his incredible college career, Jones was picked sixth overall in the 2011 NFL draft by the Atlanta Falcons. In his first season in the NFL, he started at wide receiver for the Falcons and recorded almost a thousand yards receiving with eight touchdowns. He followed that up with over a thousand yards and ten touchdowns in his second season and was selected to his first pro bowl. He suffered a broken foot after playing only five games in 2013 and was out for the rest of the season. He came back strong in the 2014 season posting career highs in both receptions and yards and getting selected to the pro bowl for the second time in his career. He posted career high numbers again in the 2015 season, recording the second most receiving yards ever in a single season in the NFL while also breaking the Falcons record for yards in a single season. This trend of excellent play has carried over into 2016. Through ten games this season, Jones is on track for another record breaking season and his fourth pro bowl appearence.

Off the Field

Everyone knows Julio Jones makes a huge impact on the football field, but not many know about the impact he makes off the field. Jones is frequently doing whatever he can to better the community around him and help those in need. He is also a great role model for all of the young football fans who look up to him by never getting in trouble off the field and always trying to positively impact his community. Julio is involved with many charities and never hesitates to attend an event whenever he can. Not only is he continuing to do his part in the community, but he is also going back to school to finish his degree at alabama. Julio Jones is an excellent member of society and has a very future on and off the football field. He is well on his way to the NFL Hall of Fame. Hopefully we will have the oppurtunity to watch Julio play for many more years, but his football career wil eventually end and all we can hope for until then is that he helps bring a Super Bowl Championship to Atlanta.
